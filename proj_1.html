Miracle Ticket

Venues across America are facing a huge problem with a disconnect between ticketing systems and the venues themselves, venues are not able to connect with their customers needs and wants. When it comes to tickets, many venues face the problem of failing to reach their market audiences and main hardcore fans due, to the cumbersome ticketing software in today's market. Major players in digital ticketing service industry, such as eventbrite and ticketmaster dominate the market with exorbitant fees and complicated ticketing systems that at their core are anti-consumer. As my senoir design project my team and I tried to combate this with our product, Miracle Ticket.
We aim to provide a straightforward ticketing solution for venues via a fully customizable and integratable software solution. By streamlining the ticket delivery process we remove unnecessary costs undergone by venues, lowering the costs to consumers. Having an entirely digital ticketing system accessible through our progressive web app with a clean user interface with a focus on ticket accessibility for the consumers and our secure QR ticket validation system for venues allows us to benefit venues and consumers?
Tickets are entirely unique to the event and tied to the venue allowing for secure management of total ticket allotment. This allows the venues to validate each ticket has been used once removing the concern of duplicate or fake tickets being used for entry. While for consumers it removes the fear of being unable to attend an event, our system allows them to return the ticket as long as it within an hour of the event starting and if the ticket has not been used. With over 35,000 small venues in the US (including bars, music events, comedy clubs, churches) there is a huge market for ticketing needs.
